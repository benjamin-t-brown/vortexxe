Vortex: Deluxe

Controls

Direction Left/Right: Rotate Ship
Direction Down: Switch Weapon
Ctrl or Up: Accelerate
Space: Fire
Shift: Shield

Instructions

Objective

Survive and advance levels by destroying all asteroids. Try to get the high score!

The following powerups are available.

- Heart: extra life
- Candy: points, armor, shield recharge
- Canister: extra gun shot, spread gun ammo, rapid gun ammo, big gun ammo
- Bonus Multiplier: Multiplies bonus
- Stars: 1000 points or bonus points
